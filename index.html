Zoey Café


Zoey Café is a service-oriented organization that has been serving the people of Iligan City for almost seventeen (17) years. Zoey Café was created thru the idea of the booming coffee industry in the country. With this in mind, we wanted to seize the opportunity and so we made our aspiration into a reality.


Thus, we celebrated the birth of Zoey Café on July 8, 2002. We started out small just selling coffee, pastries and cakes however since the idea of a coffee shop in the city was new and not many people then appreciated coffee concoctions, we decided to create an extensive menu that not only catered sandwiches and pasta but also included full course meals.


Since then we have captured the hearts and tastes of Iliganons by introducing our continental and international menus that are not customary here in Iligan.
